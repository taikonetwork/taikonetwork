    About

    The Taiko Network (also known as the Taiko Mapping Project) is an effort to map, archive, and visualize the connections between groups and members in the Taiko community.

    The goals of the project are:

    • to develop a comprehensive and accurate resource for the Taiko community,
    • to document the continuing growth of Taiko,
    • and to explore the social connectivity of the Taiko community as a result of its unique historical development.
